Off The Worldly Road

Color Palette Study

Three directions for a homepage that feels rugged enough for the wilderness and peaceful enough for the soul. Each palette is built for light/dark modes, photography-forward layouts, and quiet faith-forward tone.

Option 1

Canyon & Candlelight

Warm desert stone, burnt clay, and muted gold. Rugged like red-rock canyons at golden hour; peaceful like sanctuary light. Best for: bold photography, sun-drenched landscapes, warm personality.
